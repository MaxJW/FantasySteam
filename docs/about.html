FantasySteam

About

Fantasy Steam League is a year-long fantasy game where you draft upcoming Steam releases and compete with friends based on real-world performance.

What is Fantasy Steam League?

Create or join a private league, enter live snake drafts across three seasonal phases, and pick your Hits, Bombs, and Seasonal games. Scores update twice daily based on each game's real Steam performance: reviews, concurrent players, and growth.

The player with the highest total score at the end of the season wins.

How It Works

Each season runs for one calendar year. Draft in December (Winter), April (Summer), and August (Fall). Your picks earn points from launch hype, sales estimates, peak CCU, and review scores. Milestone bonuses reward breakout hits; bomb picks penalise underperformers.

Data Sources

Fantasy Steam League uses data from the following third-party services. Game covers and metadata are attributed to their respective providers.

IGDB (Twitch)

Game metadata, cover images, genres, and company information are sourced from IGDB . Cover images are served from images.igdb.com .

Steam

Review counts and concurrent player (CCU) data come from Steam. Game pages link to the Steam store for each title.

Firebase (Google)

Authentication (Sign in with Google) and database storage (leagues, teams, picks, scores) are provided by Firebase .

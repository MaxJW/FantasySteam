What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and keep you signed in.

Lawful basis: These cookies are necessary for the performance of the contract (providing the service you requested) and for our legitimate interest in securing your session. Under UK PECR, strictly necessary cookies do not require prior consent.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note: blocking our authentication cookies will prevent you from signing in and using the service.
